Seasonal Work

Spring

Spring is all about revitalizing your garden with new plants, lawn aeration, and a thorough garden cleanup to prepare for the year ahead.

Summer

Summer focuses on maintaining lush greenery with regular lawn mowing, consistent watering, and pruning to keep your garden vibrant.

Autumn

Autumn involves leaf clearing, preparing garden beds for the colder months, and planting bulbs to ensure a colorful and beautiful spring.

Winter

Winter is the time for tree care, frost protection for plants, and clearing or graveling for snow where necessary to keep your garden safe and tidy.
